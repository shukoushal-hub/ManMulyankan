Our Mission

Why Man Mulyankan Exists

In a world where mental health concerns often go unnoticed until they become severe, Man Mulyankan bridges the critical gap between awareness and action by making standardised screening tools freely accessible online.

The platform makes validated screening tests easily accessible, allowing anyone to complete them from home in a simple, guided format. Each assessment is transformed from a complex clinical procedure into a clear, intuitive experience — no confusing medical forms, no intimidating clinical environments.

The goal is to promote early recognition of mental health issues and cognitive difficulties, reduce stigma around psychological evaluation, encourage proactive self-awareness, and enable timely intervention — because early detection can save lives.
